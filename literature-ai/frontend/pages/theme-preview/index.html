Zhang, W.; Li, H.; Chen, Y. — Journal of Catalysis, 2024

Comprehensive Analysis

This study employs DFT calculations to investigate the electrocatalytic CO2 reduction reaction (CO2RR) on single-atom nickel catalysts supported on nitrogen-doped graphene. The computational framework includes Bader charge analysis, free energy diagrams, and ab initio molecular dynamics simulations to validate catalyst stability under operating conditions.

Key DFT Findings

The Ni-N4 site exhibits a limiting potential of -0.59 V for CO2-to-CO conversion, with a calculated overpotential significantly lower than that of conventional Ni(111) surfaces. The *COOH intermediate formation is identified as the rate-determining step.
